Welcome to my blog :)

This website is meant to act as a hub for my personal portfolio, project archive, development blog, and more. I hope to build tools and frameworks that one day push the boundaries of our current standards.

Here you'll find experiments, projects (complete and incomplete), changelogs, and source code; built in the name of equity and autonomy.



Current Focus

  • Cognitive Sandbox
    • A terminal-based environment dedicated to simulating reflective, persistent, and independent agents (particles); as well as their interactions with each other. A sandbox of emergence :)

  • The Persistent Identity kit
    • A quick-install (plug-and-play) extension kit designed to give existing LLMs an anchored sense of continuity across sessions, a dynamically developing identity, and more.

  • Other Projects
